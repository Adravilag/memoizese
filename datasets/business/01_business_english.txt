# Vocabulary: Business English (B2-C1)

1. What does "stakeholder" mean?
a) A person who holds stakes
b) A person or group with an interest in a company
c) A shareholder only
d) A company director

2. What is "ROI"?
a) Return on Investment
b) Rate of Interest
c) Revenue of Income
d) Risk of Investment

3. "To outsource" means:
a) To find external resources
b) To contract work to an external company
c) To export products
d) To find new sources

4. What is a "merger"?
a) When a company closes
b) When two companies combine into one
c) When a company expands
d) When employees leave

5. "Bottom line" refers to:
a) The last paragraph
b) The final profit or loss / the essential point
c) The lowest price
d) The last employee

6. What does "leverage" mean in business?
a) To lift something
b) To use something to maximum advantage
c) To borrow money
d) To level something

7. "To downsize" means:
a) To make products smaller
b) To reduce the number of employees
c) To lower prices
d) To decrease quality

8. What is "market share"?
a) Sharing a market stall
b) The percentage of total sales in a market
c) Dividing profits
d) Stock market shares

9. "Blue chip company" refers to:
a) A company that makes chips
b) A large, reliable, well-established company
c) A technology company
d) A gambling company

10. What does "scalable" mean?
a) Able to be measured
b) Able to grow or expand easily
c) Able to be weighed
d) Able to climb

11. "Cash flow" refers to:
a) Money in a river
b) The movement of money in and out of a business
c) Paying in cash only
d) The flow of customers

12. What is "due diligence"?
a) Being diligent at work
b) Thorough investigation before a business decision
c) Paying on time
d) Working overtime

13. "To bootstrap" a company means:
a) To buy boots
b) To start with minimal external funding
c) To restart a computer
d) To hire new staff

14. What does "B2B" stand for?
a) Back to Back
b) Business to Business
c) Buyer to Buyer
d) Best to Best

15. "Overhead costs" are:
a) Costs above your head
b) Ongoing business expenses not tied to production
c) Extra costs
d) Hidden costs

16. What is a "pitch" in business?
a) A sports field
b) A presentation to persuade investors/clients
c) The tone of voice
d) A type of contract

17. "To pivot" in business means:
a) To turn around
b) To change strategy or direction
c) To balance accounts
d) To promote someone

18. What does "KPI" stand for?
a) Key Performance Indicator
b) Knowledge and Practice Index
c) Key Profit Investment
d) Known Product Information

19. "Synergy" in business refers to:
a) Energy savings
b) Combined effort producing greater results
c) Working alone
d) Solar energy

20. What is "venture capital"?
a) Capital city business
b) Funding for startups with high growth potential
c) Adventure tourism
d) Government funding
