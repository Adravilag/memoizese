# Grammar: Future Tenses (B1-B2)

1. Which tense is used for scheduled events?
a) Will + infinitive
b) Present Simple
c) Going to
d) Future Continuous

2. "The train ___ at 9:00 AM." (timetable)
a) will leave
b) leaves
c) is going to leave
d) is leaving

3. When do we use "going to"?
a) For spontaneous decisions
b) For plans and intentions already decided
c) For timetables
d) For promises

4. "I think it ___ tomorrow." (prediction without evidence)
a) rains
b) is raining
c) will rain
d) is going to rain

5. "Look at those clouds! It ___." (prediction with evidence)
a) will rain
b) is going to rain
c) rains
d) is raining

6. Future Continuous is used for:
a) Completed future actions
b) Actions in progress at a specific future time
c) Timetables
d) Promises

7. "This time tomorrow, I ___ on the beach."
a) will lie
b) will be lying
c) am lying
d) lie

8. "I promise I ___ you." (promise)
a) am helping
b) help
c) will help
d) am going to help

9. Future Perfect expresses:
a) An action in progress in the future
b) An action completed before a specific future time
c) A planned action
d) A spontaneous decision

10. "By next year, I ___ my degree."
a) will finish
b) will have finished
c) am finishing
d) finish

11. "What ___ this weekend?" (asking about plans)
a) will you do
b) do you do
c) are you doing
d) you do

12. Which is a spontaneous decision?
a) I'm meeting John at 5.
b) The plane leaves at 8.
c) I'll answer the phone.
d) I'm going to study medicine.

13. "By the time you arrive, I ___."
a) will leave
b) will have left
c) am leaving
d) leave

14. Future Perfect Continuous emphasizes:
a) A completed action
b) Duration of an action up to a point in the future
c) A timetable
d) A sudden decision

15. "By December, I ___ here for 10 years."
a) will work
b) will be working
c) will have been working
d) am working

16. "The concert ___ at 8 PM." (scheduled event)
a) will start
b) is going to start
c) starts
d) is starting

17. We use "shall" mainly for:
a) Predictions
b) Offers and suggestions (Shall I...?)
c) Plans
d) Timetables

18. "___ I open the window?"
a) Will
b) Shall
c) Am
d) Do

19. "I ___ 30 next month." (stating a fact about the future)
a) am being
b) will be
c) am going to be
d) Both B and C

20. "Don't call at 8. I ___ dinner."
a) will have
b) will be having
c) have
d) am going to have
