# Grammar: Passive and Active Voice (B1-C1)

1. "The letter ___ by the secretary yesterday."
a) wrote
b) was written
c) has written
d) is writing

2. Which sentence is in the passive voice?
a) She writes novels.
b) The novel was written by her.
c) She has written many novels.
d) She is writing a novel.

3. "They ___ the new bridge next year." (Active)
a) will build
b) will be built
c) are built
d) were building

4. Change to passive: "Someone stole my car."
a) My car stolen.
b) My car was stolen.
c) My car is stolen.
d) My car has stolen.

5. "The cake ___ (make) by my grandmother every Sunday."
a) makes
b) is made
c) made
d) has made

6. Which tense is used: "The report has been submitted"?
a) Present Simple Passive
b) Present Perfect Passive
c) Past Simple Passive
d) Future Passive

7. "English ___ in many countries around the world."
a) speaks
b) is spoken
c) spoke
d) has spoken

8. Change to active: "The window was broken by the children."
a) The children broke the window.
b) The children was breaking the window.
c) The children breaks the window.
d) The children have broke the window.

9. "The project ___ (complete) by the team by Friday."
a) will complete
b) will be completed
c) is completing
d) completes

10. Which sentence is INCORRECT?
a) The house was built in 1990.
b) The homework was done by me.
c) The car was drove by John.
d) The letter was sent yesterday.

11. "The suspect ___ (question) by the police right now."
a) questions
b) is being questioned
c) questioned
d) was questioned

12. "The results ___ (announce) tomorrow morning."
a) announce
b) will be announced
c) announced
d) are announcing

13. Which verb CANNOT be used in passive voice?
a) write
b) happen
c) make
d) give

14. "My wallet ___ (steal). I can't find it anywhere."
a) stole
b) has been stolen
c) is stealing
d) steals

15. Change to passive: "People speak Spanish in Mexico."
a) Spanish is spoken in Mexico.
b) Spanish spoken in Mexico.
c) Spanish speaks in Mexico.
d) Spanish was spoken in Mexico.

16. "The meeting ___ (hold) in the conference room yesterday."
a) held
b) was held
c) is held
d) holds

17. "The new policy ___ (implement) since January."
a) implemented
b) has been implemented
c) was implementing
d) implements

18. Which is the correct passive form of "They are building a new school"?
a) A new school is built.
b) A new school is being built.
c) A new school was built.
d) A new school has been built.

19. "The documents ___ (must/sign) before the deadline."
a) must sign
b) must be signed
c) must signed
d) must to be signed

20. "The criminal ___ (arrest) while he was trying to escape."
a) arrested
b) was arrested
c) has arrested
d) is arrested
