# Idioms: Common English Idioms (B1-B2)

1. What does "a piece of cake" mean?
a) A dessert
b) Something very easy
c) A small portion
d) A celebration

2. "Break the ice" means:
a) To freeze something
b) To start a conversation in a social situation
c) To break something
d) To cool down

3. What does "hit the nail on the head" mean?
a) To hurt yourself
b) To be exactly right
c) To use a hammer
d) To make a mistake

4. "Once in a blue moon" means:
a) Every night
b) Very rarely
c) During full moon
d) Often

5. What does "cost an arm and a leg" mean?
a) To be injured
b) To be very expensive
c) To be cheap
d) To need surgery

6. "Under the weather" means:
a) Outside in the rain
b) Feeling ill or unwell
c) Checking the forecast
d) Happy

7. What does "beat around the bush" mean?
a) To garden
b) To avoid saying something directly
c) To win a competition
d) To exercise outdoors

8. "Let the cat out of the bag" means:
a) To free a cat
b) To reveal a secret
c) To go shopping
d) To pack a bag

9. What does "kill two birds with one stone" mean?
a) To hunt birds
b) To accomplish two things with one action
c) To throw stones
d) To waste time

10. "Bite the bullet" means:
a) To eat metal
b) To face a difficult situation bravely
c) To be aggressive
d) To avoid problems

11. What does "spill the beans" mean?
a) To cook badly
b) To reveal secret information
c) To make a mess
d) To plant seeds

12. "The ball is in your court" means:
a) To play tennis
b) It's your decision/turn to act
c) To lose something
d) To be in court

13. What does "burn the midnight oil" mean?
a) To waste resources
b) To work late into the night
c) To cook at midnight
d) To have insomnia

14. "A blessing in disguise" means:
a) A costume
b) Something bad that turns out to be good
c) A religious ceremony
d) A hidden gift

15. What does "get out of hand" mean?
a) To drop something
b) To lose control of a situation
c) To escape
d) To give away

16. "Speak of the devil" is used when:
a) Talking about evil
b) Someone you were just talking about appears
c) Being scared
d) Telling a lie

17. What does "on the same page" mean?
a) Reading together
b) Having the same understanding or opinion
c) Writing a book
d) In the same place

18. "The best of both worlds" means:
a) Traveling globally
b) Having advantages of two different situations
c) Being wealthy
d) Having two homes

19. What does "it's not rocket science" mean?
a) It's about space
b) It's not difficult to understand
c) It's scientific
d) It's impossible

20. "Actions speak louder than words" means:
a) To be quiet
b) What you do is more important than what you say
c) To shout
d) To act in a play
