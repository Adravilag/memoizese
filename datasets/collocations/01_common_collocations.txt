# Vocabulary: Collocations (B2-C1)

1. What collocates with "make" - a decision or a choice?
a) Only "a decision"
b) Both "a decision" and "a choice"
c) Only "a choice"
d) Neither

2. Which is correct: "do homework" or "make homework"?
a) Make homework
b) Do homework
c) Both are correct
d) Neither is correct

3. "Heavy" collocates with:
a) Rain, traffic, smoker
b) Rain, cars, cigarettes
c) Water, vehicles, tobacco
d) Storm, jam, addict

4. Which verb collocates with "a mistake"?
a) Do
b) Make
c) Have
d) Take

5. "Strong" collocates with:
a) Tea, coffee, accent
b) Tea, coffee, voice
c) Drink, beverage, sound
d) Liquid, caffeine, tone

6. Which is correct: "take a photo" or "make a photo"?
a) Make a photo
b) Take a photo
c) Both are correct
d) Do a photo

7. "Fast" collocates with:
a) Food, asleep, car
b) Food, asleep, train
c) Meal, sleep, vehicle
d) Burger, nap, automobile

8. Which verb goes with "an effort"?
a) Do
b) Make
c) Have
d) Give

9. "Bitterly" collocates with:
a) Cold, disappointed, crying
b) Cold, disappointed, complain
c) Freezing, upset, weeping
d) Chilly, sad, sobbing

10. Which is correct: "have a rest" or "make a rest"?
a) Make a rest
b) Have a rest
c) Do a rest
d) Take a rest (also correct)

11. "Catch" collocates with:
a) A cold, fire, someone's eye

12. Which verb goes with "progress"?
a) Do

13. "Highly" collocates with:

14. Which is correct: "do the dishes" or "make the dishes"?
a) Make the dishes

15. "Pay" collocates with:
a) Attention, a visit, a compliment

16. Which verb goes with "an appointment"?
a) Do

a) Make responsibility

a) A conclusion, an end, terms with

20. Which verb goes with "a living"?
a) Do
